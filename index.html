Greetings!

Hello there! My objective for this web page is to highlight the key projects I made.

I work as a Professional Hardware Design Engineer. In my professional career I've been in the electronics design industry for more than 6 years now. I have extensive experience in building multiple projects gearing towards Internet-of-things(IOT), Power electronics, and Electric Vehicles. Some of the recent projects I am involved in are the home water control and monitoring using IMX6 as the main MPU, co-developed the hardware validation and verification of a Smart Fuse Circuit Breaker, design lead of the Generator Efficiency Optimizer Project, and much more.

Protocols I used on my previous projects are MODBUS, SPI, I2C, UART, ZWAVE and DALI. The main CAD software I use in Schematic and PCB layouts are KiCAD, Altium, and Cadence. For circuit simulation and analysis, I use LTSpice.

As for my firmware development experience, I mainly use C language. The platforms I use are Arduino, PlatformIO and Zephyr. You can find the programs I made on my github account: https://github.com/bryndelltorio/

The most recent project I’ve been involved in is an electric motor conversion kit. We are converting a gasoline-powered motorcycle into a fully electric motorbike. I lead the electrical design of this project by creating the wiring harness, which connects all the main devices such as gauge, break switch, throttle, 48V battery, and lighting circuitry. This prototype runs for about 3 hours at an average speed of 60 kph.
